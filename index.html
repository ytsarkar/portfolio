About Me

I'm a passionate graphic designer with a keen eye for detail and a love for creating visual stories. My journey in design began when I was just 15, doodling logos for imaginary companies in my school notebooks.

Today, I combine strategic thinking with creative execution to deliver designs that not only look beautiful but also serve their purpose effectively. I believe good design should be both aesthetically pleasing and functional.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring new coffee shops, attending design conferences, or teaching design principles to aspiring creatives.
